While web pages may not look exactly as you're used to, its architecture is similar to that of large commercial browsers.

It can:

  • Make HTTP and HTTPS requests;
  • Parse HTML;
  • Distinguish different types of elements;
  • Apply styles to page elements;


This project was heavily inspired by the online book Web Browser Engineering, which I highly recommend reading. It demonstrates that even the most complex systems can be broken down into components and understood by anyone.
